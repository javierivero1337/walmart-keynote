# Design Brief & Slide Outline: "The Human Stack"

Instead of dictating strict layouts, this brief gives the designer the emotional vibe, the narrative contrast, and the visual metaphors so they can let their creativity run wild.

---

## Creative Direction for the Designer

**The Emotional Arc:** The presentation is divided into two distinct acts.

- **Opening (Slides 1–2):** Title + personal intro. Elegant, high-contrast opener, then a human beat before the narrative kicks in.
- **Act I (Slides 3–7):** Dark, stark, high-contrast, data-heavy, slightly intense. Represents the "exponential shockwaves" of the AI wave.
- **Act II (Slides 8–13):** Warm, inviting, human, organic, and minimalist. Represents the transition into the "Human Stack"—focusing on human agency and soul over tech machinery.

**The Rule:** Keep slides highly visual. Avoid text-heavy lists. Use bold typography and metaphors rather than literal icons of robots or microchips.

---

## Slide 1: Title Slide — Something Big IS Happening

**The Core Message:** Set the stage. A historic, unignorable shift is underway.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** A striking, cinematic title screen. Think of a physical "shift" or "split"—a subtle line, a horizon, or a beautifully textured split-screen that represents a clear "Before" and "After" in human history. High contrast.

**Vibe:** Dark, elegant, intriguing.

## Slide 3: The Catalyst — The Pivot Point

**The Core Message:** Just like in 2020, we woke up one day to a world operating under entirely new rules.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** Abstract representations of acceleration or disruption. A visual metaphor of a ripple effect, a wave breaking, or a timeline with a sharp bend. Avoid cliché images of viruses; instead, focus on the raw energy of "sudden, global shift."

**Vibe:** Stark, dramatic, immediate.

---

## Slide 4: La Curva Exponencial

**The Core Message:** AI capability isn't creeping forward—it's compounding. One exponential curve, six years, six inflection points that rewrote what's possible.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** A single hero chart spanning **2021 → 2026**. The Y-axis is **intelligence / capability** (not revenue—this is about what machines can *do*). The curve starts nearly flat and goes vertical by 2026. Each year is marked by **one milestone dot** on the curve—large enough to read from the back of the room, with the year and a short label.


| Year     | Milestone (editable)                                                               |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **2021** | GPT-3 & Codex — language models cross into general usefulness                      |
| **2022** | ChatGPT — 100M users in 2 months; AI goes mainstream overnight                     |
| **2023** | GPT-4 — multimodal leap; models begin passing professional exams                   |
| **2024** | Reasoning era (o1) + agentic AI — models that plan, tool-use, and chain steps      |
| **2025** | Autonomous agents at scale — AI that operates software, workflows, and teams       |
| **2026** | *[Current frontier — edit: e.g. near-human performance on complex knowledge work]* |


**Layout notes:**

- Title on slide: **"La curva exponencial"** — bold, minimal supporting copy.
- The curve is the protagonist; milestone labels sit at each year tick on the X-axis.
- Optional click-through: reveal one milestone per click (2021 → 2026), with the curve drawing further each beat—or show the full curve at once with milestones lighting up sequentially.
- Keep milestone text to **one line each**. No paragraphs.

**Vibe:** Minimalist, data-driven, awe-inducing. The audience should feel the acceleration before the company-specific charts on the next slide.

## Slide 5: The Velocity — Exponential Reality

**The Core Message:** The insane, vertical adoption speeds of OpenAI, Anthropic, and Cursor. This isn't a future trend; it has already arrived.

**Interaction (click-through):** One slide, three beats. Each click advances to the next company's growth chart—no page change, just a reveal. The audience should feel the same vertical shock three times in a row.


| Click       | Company       | What appears                                                                                     |
| ----------- | ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1 (initial) | **Cursor**    | Growth chart #1 — adoption / ARR / developer growth. The steepest or most dramatic of the three. |
| 2           | **OpenAI**    | Growth chart #2 — revenue, users, or API volume over time. Steep exponential curve.              |
| 3           | **Anthropic** | Growth chart #3 — same metric style as OpenAI for visual consistency. Equally vertical.          |


**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** A single, full-bleed data canvas. One hero chart at a time—large, cinematic, impossible to ignore. Each chart should share the same layout language (axes, typography, color accent) so the comparison lands instantly; only the company name and data change on click.

- **Not** three small charts side by side on load.
- **Not** a boring static bar chart—think gravity-defying curves, rocket-trajectory lines, or a stark linear-vs-exponential contrast baked into each chart.
- Optional: a subtle company label or logo that cross-fades on each click. Keep the focus on the curve itself.

**Data notes for the designer:**

- Use real or realistic growth data where possible (public revenue estimates, user milestones, funding rounds as proxy).
- Each chart should tell one story: "This went vertical, fast."
- Consider a shared baseline or ghost line showing "typical SaaS growth" so the exponential gap reads at a glance.

**Vibe:** Minimalist, data-driven, breathtaking. Let the numbers do the talking—minimal copy on screen beyond the company name and one headline stat per click.

## Slide 6: The Paradox — AlphaGo Zero

**The Core Message:** When AI learned from human data, it was limited. When it learned on its own, it became untouchable.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** Contrasting visual systems. One side representing rigid, structured, linear human logic (grids, old blueprints). The other side representing organic, complex, hyper-efficient AI logic (flowing nodes, multi-dimensional structures).

**Vibe:** Sci-fi, clean, highly conceptual.

---

## Slide 7: The Corporate Trap — The Tech Stack Illusion

**The Core Message:** Companies are obsessing over the wrong thing. They are panicking about which AI tool to use.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** Chaos vs. Clarity. A messy "landscape" or "cloud" of logos and software icons fading into a single question mark. A visual sensory overload representing the noise of the tech market.

**Vibe:** Monochromatic, slightly chaotic, leading to a visual pause.

---

## Slide 8: The Pivot — The Silicon vs. The Human Stack

**The Core Message:** Shift the room's energy. Stop looking at the silicon chips; look at the humans in the seats.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** This is the bridge. Transition the slide palette here from cold/dark to warm/cream tones. A beautiful, striking typography slide. Maybe a simple diagram showing a massive tech layer ending at a single, glowing "human core."

**Vibe:** Calming, grounding, spacious.

---

## Slide 9: Pillar 1 — Curiosidad

**The Core Message:** When answers are free and infinite, asking beautiful, daring questions is the only value left.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** Exploration and looking beyond borders. An abstract path winding into the unknown, a prism splitting light, or a minimalist lens focusing on a single point.

**Vibe:** Warm, open, thoughtful.

---

## Slide 10: Pillar 2 — Iniciativa

**The Core Message:** You are no longer just an operator; you are the Director.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** Sovereignty and direction. A visual metaphor of a compass, a hand shaping raw material, or a single figure standing at the helm of a massive, beautiful horizon. Focus on the concept of intent and control.

**Vibe:** Empowering, grand, cinematic.

---

## Slide 11: Pillar 3 — Optimismo

**The Core Message:** Fear freezes you. Optimism is a calculated, strategic choice to leverage the storm instead of hiding from it.

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** Warm, radiating energy. Think gradients of gold, peach, and sunbursts. A visual metaphor of a sail catching a heavy wind, or a seed breaking through hard soil.

**Vibe:** Bright, vibrant, active, inspiring.

---

## Slide 12: The Synthesis — The Wind at Your Back

**The Core Message:** "La IA no viene a reemplazarte, viene a preguntarte quién vas a ser cuando ya no tengas que hacer el trabajo aburrido."

**Vibe:** Balanced, harmonious, powerful.

---

## Slide 13: Outro — Enjoy the Ride

**The Core Message:** "Alguien con una curiosidad insaciable, una iniciativa absoluta y sobre todo un optimismo radical"

**Visual Concept & Metaphor:** An open, expansive horizon or a beautiful, abstract path disappearing into a bright future. Clean, high-impact typography with your closing sign-off.

**Vibe:** Elevating, memorable, breathing room.
